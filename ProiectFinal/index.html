STAGE What does it mean?
Meeting discussion with the beneficiary to get to know his tastes and needs.
The survey measuring and knowing in detail the space to be arranged - dimensioned plan; identifying assets, photographing the space so as not to miss any detail.
Establishing the budget. ----------------
Layout options our interior designer will work with the client from start to finish. 2-3 furnishing options will be proposed, through a 2D plan.
Concept "moodboards" will be created - containing materials, products from the arrangement to feel the atmosphere of the place and the way the elements combine.
Three-dimensional images (3D) following the choice made by the client (2D plan/concept) under the guidance of the designer, the final solution is arrived at, which will translate into 3D simulations.
Estimated estimate based on the budget, lists of furniture items at fair prices with quality products, a work plan and technical details: electrical plan, fasteners and material joints, etc.
Technical details furniture sketches, electrical plan, fasteners and joints, electrical plan, tile and tile assembly, ceiling and floor plans.
Product orders we carry out and track all orders for furniture, accessories, household appliances, etc. We make visits with or for the client, to showrooms to choose tiles, parquet, doors and everything necessary for furnishing. Soon the space will be personalized and loaded with value.
Site follow-up together with the craftsman team to explain the technical drawings to them and ensure that everything can be done on time and as planned. We will return throughout the execution period to check the progress of the works.
